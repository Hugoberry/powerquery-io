## Remarks

The Teams Analytics connector enables you to get insights into your usage of Teams. Data is available for both your personal activity in Teams as well as the activity in Teams you are a member of.<br />Data is available for prior 90 days. <br /> For details of the types of data available, please refer to the following help documentation: <br /><ul><li><b>Teams Usage:</b> https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/teams-analytics-and-reports/teams-usage-report</li><li><b>Teams User Activity:</b> https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/teams-analytics-and-reports/user-activity-report</li></ul>
